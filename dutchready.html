Dutch Ready

Date / 2017
Client / Dutch Ready
Visit site

Dutch Ready is an Dutch tutoring company that offers private Dutch lessons to Amsterdam’s ever-growing expat community. Together with the founder, I helped define the service and built a web experience that communicated the company’s values and the benefits of learning the language.

We started with a competitive analysis to gain an understanding of the current market and demand for private tutoring in the Netherlands. Conversations with the target audience revealed that timing was the main hurdle for many expats who discontinued lessons. Learning a language demands a great deal of time and energy, both of which working expats rarely have. Therefore, we positioned Dutch Ready as a company that comes to the student, and works around the student’s schedule, week by week. Dutch Ready uses a unique methodolgy, which was an important key factor to convey through the website.

I started with sketches to explore a range of possible information architectures and visual styles, to see what spoke best to the user base. We synthesized the best of each direction into a clear design system that brought focus to the student’s experiences through simple illustrations.

I built the site using Bootstrap 4 as a basis, refining details and copy along the way. We launch an MVP version of the site and continued to test illustration styles and content to see what brought the highest traffic and conversion rates.
