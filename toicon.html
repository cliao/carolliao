Iconography

to [icon]

Date / 2014-ongoing
Client / The Artificial
Visit site

to [icon] is a resource for designers that contains free pixel-perfect icon sets, based on verbs and executed with whimsy. What began as a Kickstarter campaign quickly reified into a fully fledged icon depository that now contains over 2000 pixel-perfect icons. Unlike other icon design ventures, to [icon] focuses solely on verbs. Actions speak louder than words, and these action icons aim to help better express ideas.

I contributed 3 series each containing at least 150 icons – Hatch, Fandom, and Duotone. All our icons are free to download and use under the Creative Commons Attribution license.

Each series consists of carefully crafted pixel-perfect icons that are designed with its own unique rules to ensure consistency and visual balance.

In addition to icon design, I assisted with the site design and development, as well as with creating promotional materials and products such as posters, stickers, buttons, and more.

In 2016 I presented to [icon] at The Volkshotel in a Pecha Kucha style lecture series called The Hmm.
